Selling copper cable in Odessa — is it worth it?

Yes! Insulated copper cable is one of the most common scrap types. The price depends on the copper content percentage. We determine this with an analyser on site and pay a fair price. No need to strip the insulation yourself — bring it as is.

Tips for selling copper at the best price

  • Sort your scrap by type — bright copper separate, mix separate, cable separate
  • Don't burn cable — burnt copper costs less than insulated cable
  • Larger volumes mean better prices: from 1 tonne we pick up for free
